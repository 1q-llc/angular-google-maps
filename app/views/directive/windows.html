The windows directive is used to display multiple InfoWindow objects on your map. It has a performance advantage over the window directive since you don't have to rely on ng-repeat to instantiate the multiple objects.

Usage

<windows
	show='{expression}'
	closeClick='{expression}'>
	...
</windows>
